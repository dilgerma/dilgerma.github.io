## Overview

This tab is typically your starting point if you want to navigate the model quickly, access additional information like thedata trail analysis or general configuraiton like AI Tokens for your account.

### Activating your license

To activate your license, send an email to info@nebulit.de with your Miro Account ( 17 ). If you bought a License, it will get activated within just a few hours.
